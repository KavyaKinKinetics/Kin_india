Can AI Predict Astronaut Health Risks?

Astronauts on long-duration missions face unknown health risks, especially from radiation exposure. Traditional risk models struggle to capture long-term effects—but AI-driven survival analysis is changing the game.

Introducing COLOSSUS, a new survival analysis tool that:
  • Uses machine learning to predict radiation-related health outcomes.
  • Improves astronaut risk assessment for long-term missions.
  • Helps develop protective countermeasures for deep-space travel.

Why this matters:

  • COLOSSUS could revolutionize medical risk prediction for astronauts.
  • AI-driven survival models benefit cancer research and military medicine.
  • Predictive analytics ensures safer, data-driven space missions.

As we move toward long-duration human spaceflight, data-driven AI models will be key to astronaut health and decision-making. Could AI-based risk modelling be the next breakthrough in space medicine?
