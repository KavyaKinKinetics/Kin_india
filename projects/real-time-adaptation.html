21st Reinventing Space Conference

Hosted by the British Interplanetary Society at the Royal Aeronautical Society, Mayfair.

KinKinetics had an incredible experience at the 21st Reinventing Space Conference, hosted by the British Interplanetary Society at the Royal Aeronautical Society in Mayfair. The three-day event was packed with engaging sessions, including the prestigious Gala Dinner and Arthur Awards. The highlight of the conference for us was participating in hands-on activities like the Soyuz controller demo, which allowed attendees to practice dockingâ€”a unique and fun experience!

One of the most inspiring aspects of the event was being part of the "Voice of the Next Generation" workshop. This session brought together talented young professionals and students to discuss the future of the UK space sector and sustainable space management. Representing KinKinetics, we had the chance to collaborate with like-minded peers and contribute fresh ideas to shape the next chapter of space exploration.

In a dynamic and collaborative environment, our team created a well-received presentation within just one hour, showcasing innovative solutions to challenges in near-Earth space management. Working alongside visionary young thinkers like Ethan Jones, Robert Lynn, Harsimret Singh Dhami, George L., Emmy (Elif) Keve, MSc, MEng, PhD Candidate, and Michele Marino, KinKinetics demonstrated its commitment to advancing sustainability in space exploration.

KinKinetics would like to extend a heartfelt thank you to Swetha Kotichintala, Joanna Hart, and Evandros Theodosiou, as well as everyone else who made this event possible. Special thanks to Mr. Gerald D. Griffin for his inspiring address. This conference was a celebration of the innovation, passion, and community that drive the future of the space sector. We are proud to be part of this vibrant and dynamic industry.
