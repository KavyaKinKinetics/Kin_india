KinKinetics at Ignite Space 2025

Driving Innovation in Space Psychology

KinKinetics had the incredible opportunity to be part of Ignite Space 2025, held at the National Space Centre in Leicester! This prestigious event, hosted by the UK Space Agency, brought together the brightest minds and businesses pushing the boundaries of the space industry.

As a company dedicated to enhancing psychological resilience and decision-making in high-stakes environments, KinKinetics was honored to connect with experts, innovators, and potential collaborators who share our vision for the future of human spaceflight.

Key Takeaways from Ignite Space 2025:

  • Strengthening partnerships with industry leaders to integrate neuroeconomics and AI into astronaut support systems.
  • Exploring new applications for KinKinetics technology in deep space missions, remote research stations, and high-pressure environments.
  • Gaining insights into the latest advancements in space medicine, behavioral science, and AI-driven decision support.

Our KinKinetics poster was among the exhibits that garnered significant attention. The concept of neuroeconomic-based decision support for long-duration missions resonated with many attendees, sparking insightful discussions on the practical applications of our technology.

This event reinforced our commitment to supporting astronauts, researchers, and professionals in extreme environments, ensuring that the future of space exploration is not only technologically advanced but also human-centered.

A huge thank you to the UK Space Agency and all the amazing minds we had the chance to engage with. We’re excited for what’s next!

Stay tuned for more updates as we continue our journey at the intersection of space, psychology, and AI!
